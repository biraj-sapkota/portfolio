Technology Impacts

With the passage of time, technology is also becoming advance. The recent world is running due to the modern technology. This advancement of technology had brought a lot of pros as well as cons to humans. All the nations and public are seeking for more advance technology. Life style of people are changing drastically due to the invoke of modern technology. No one wants to stay behind from technology. All wants to be ahead in using technology devices or gadgets for ease and comfort lifestyle. And it’s true that if one gets away from technology he/ she couldn’t compete with others in any field or areas and might have hard times.


The modern banks, hospitals, share market and even several groceries are executed with the use of modern technology. In the near days, the use of paper notes might be disappeared like as the coins today. This advancement had brought people’s life to heaven. Today, everyone is interconnected every time worldwide. This impact has made a positive impact on our daily life that no one needs to work hard physically in order to survive. Focusing on context of students, technology had helped in their reading and learning processes. Talking about now, the severe rise of the worldwide pandemic of COVID-19 had affected many fields including education. But the technology had made an alternate way for the learning processes. Students can attend the classes right at their home through several means. They can fully revise and go through the content afterwards as well through recorded session videos.


Moving on students can research for several new things online. They could enhance their knowledge on newer things. Moreover, several institutions and colleges had been providing free or low cost online classes which might be a beneficial for the students. Students can also communicate to the experts online for several reasons. Additionally, free e-library provided by many institutions can be used for reading books of desired genres. Furthermore, students could also take part in several programs or competitions being held online and get certifications as well. So, numerous benefits are there that the technology had brought in student life.


As is said there would always be a negative aspect for every good one, same things hold true for the technology as well. The vulnerable things that the technology had brought are highly affecting the people’s behaviour, physical health and many more. Lots of people are engaged in different criminal activities through the means of technology like hacking, piracy, etc. which is not a good aspect. Secondly, due to the ease that the technology has made people wants to stay within his /her room rather being exposed to the people around. Lack of physical exercise and walk has made the people suffer from several diseases. In addition to, retina of eyes is totally affected by the rays from gadgets making them depress and stressful. Due to this, many people are being mentally challenged nowadays.


To sum up, students are also suffering from the access use of devices in their physical, mental as well as social health. Small children are compelled to wear spectacles in their child age due to the excess use of gadgets and devices. Several problems like insomnia, depression and eye-problem are arising day by day. So, everyone must be concern about the harmful effects of its excessive use.
